Topic: Indoor activities is way better than outdoor activities.

I disagree with the statement that indoor activities are much better than outdoor activities.

I argue that outdoor activities is much better than Indoor activities.

People often ask, "what do you prefer to stay home with some electronic devices like smartphones, laptops, and TV or to spend time outdoor?"
It is one of the debated questions these days since the way people spend their time is different compared to the past.
People used to spend time doing outside activities such as hiking, biking, and meeting friends in the past.
However, these days, many people prefer to do indoor activities. Due to technology development,
they can use electronic devices to do many things like playing online games, watching movies, and surfing the internet.
However, these changes make people have limited enjoyment of their interests and ruin their health.
Therefore, I claim that doing outdoor activities is much better than enjoying indoor activities.

What are the advantages of outdoor activities?

People can be happier

As many people already know, doing outdoor activities is suitable for people's physical health.
However, also doing outdoor activities can help with their mental health.
Spending time, especially in nature, can improve people's mood and get rid of their stress and depression.
Imagine that you are at the park.
They definitely provide you with a place to relax.
You can also unwind and recharge while relaxing.

It gives you an opportunity to make a friend.

By spending time outdoor with others, people can experience the social benefits of outdoor activities.
For example, people can meet and build relationships with others when they participate in sports that they can enjoy with various people.

For these reasons, I argue that it is much better to spend time outdoors.
